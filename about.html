Who We Are

Crafting Stories
Worth Keeping

Jharkhand Wedding is a professional photography and videography team dedicated to capturing life's most meaningful celebrations with creativity and precision.

Based in Ranchi and operating across India, we specialize in cinematic wedding films, pre-wedding shoots, and complete event coverage including engagements, haldi, mehendi, receptions, and more.

With over 3 years of experience, our team blends traditional storytelling with modern cinematic techniques to create timeless visual narratives.

We focus on authentic, candid moments — real emotions, genuine smiles, and unforgettable memories — rather than staged poses.

"We don't just capture events — we craft stories you'll cherish forever."
